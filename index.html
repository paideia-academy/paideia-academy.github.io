WELCOME TO PAIDEIA ACADEMY

Paideia Academy is committed to providing an exceptional education based on Christian values. The school challenges each student to develop and build lasting character traits throughout a strong academic curriculum.

The beauty of Paideia is that we accept students where they actually are academically; then teach and train them to work independently and to be the possible person that they can be. In order to adequately prepare students, we use textbooks and materials that develop both mind and heart.
